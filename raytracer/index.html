Raytracer

As part of the Image Synthesis course at ETH every student wrote and extended a renderer in C++. Here I will present some of the features of my renderer, as well as images produced by it.

The above image won me the second prize in the rendering competition at the end of the course. The theme for this competition was Opposites and we had to extend our renderer with a set of self-chosen features to create an interesting image.

Some of the features I picked were Environment Lighting, Depth of Field and Colored Glass.

I also implemented Arbitrary mesh lights and materials such as Varnished diffuse and Rough dielectrics.
