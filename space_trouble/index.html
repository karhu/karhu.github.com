Space Trouble!

In spring 2012, I took the Game Programming Laboratory Course at ETH Zurich, together with Daniel Zimmermann and Rudolf M. Schreier. Over 14 weeks we envisoned, designed and coded. The result of this both intense and fun endeavor is the multiplayer game “Space Trouble!”.

Competing with 4 other projects, “Space Trouble!” won both the audience and expert price during the public presentation event at the end of the course.

2012's iteration of the Game Programming Lab was centered around the course toptic Attraction. We were coached by experts from ETH Zurich, Disney Research Zurich and Studio Gobo and lead through the various steps of developing games for the Xbox 360 platform.
